FAQ

General

  • Change your password with the 'Modify your user info' dropbown box
  • Do not use your browser's back, forward, or refresh/F5. It will cause your session to end. Use the buttons on the page to reload/navigate

Points and Bidding

  • Each time an item is upbid, the auction resets to 3 day timer. Make aggressive bids to avoid the auctions lasting a week or longer
  • Stone, ely, alps, and demonforge gear can only use points from its own zone, so if you have 100 stone points you may bid only 100 on stone gear
  • All other zones require 10% points from its own zone, so if you have 100 kalata points, you can bid 1000 points on kalata gear
  • Your 'Available' points is total points minus points tied up in bids. Points become available again if you're outibd
  • There's no auction expiry / outbid notices at this time - watch the list carefully!
  • A full Elysium Day 1 run is worth 10080 points, split among runners

Submitting runs

  • Anyone may submit a run, use 'Submit Run' page
    1. select zone you ran
    2. add names to Runners Box
    3. add eq; put everything in a container, app all container, paste into Eq Box
    4. add notes in Comments Box about who arrived late or left early, this is important to not screw people who show up first and leave last
    5. make sure to submit / save changes
    6. Send eq to Draknor
    7. An admin will verify the sent eq, update points based on who arrived late / left early, and approve the run

  • Run submission tips
    • Pick the right zone - you can't change it later and will have to delete/recreate run
    • Remember to add Comments about who came late / left early, or you screw your best people who stayed for whole thing!
    • When pasting eq, make sure each piece of eq doesn't linewrap. The system considers every new line to be a new piece of eq
    • Leaders, make sure to add yourself to runners list
