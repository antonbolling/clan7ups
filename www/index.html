Welcome!

This UPS is different. For example:
  • You may transfer, gift or sell your UPS points to other players. After all, they are your points.
  • Leaders cannot create points. Runners get a piece of a fixed points amount, like splitting a pizza.
  • Points slowly deteriorate over time (months). No more points build-up, emphasis is on "now" and new eq runs.

FAQ

Q: How do I change my password?
A: 'Modify your user info' in the dropdown box.

Q: Why is my session expiring?
A: Your session will expire if you use your browser's back/forward/refresh buttons. Use the buttons on the webpage.

Submitting runs

  • Anyone may submit a run, use the 'Submit Run' page.
  • Run submission tips
    • Pick the right zone - it cannot be changed and you will have to delete/recreate the run.
    • When pasting eq, ensure each item is on one line. Every new line is considered to be a piece of eq.
    • Use Attendance to reward players that show up on-time and stay until the very end.
